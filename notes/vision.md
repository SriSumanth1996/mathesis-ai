# Mathesis AI Vision

Mathesis AI is an intuition-first visual book for understanding the mathematics behind ML and AI.

Core belief:

You already know the intuition. Mathesis AI makes the math behind ML and AI clear.

This project will not teach math by fear, formulas, or memorization.
It will show that the ideas behind functions, change, gradients, vectors, matrices, neural networks, and embeddings are already connected to common human intuition.

The goal is to build interactive educational material where learners first see the idea, then play with it, and only then meet the formula.
